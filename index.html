Welcome to Delta Intelligence

Our Mission

Delta Intelligence advances multi-agent and continual learning for large language models (LLMs) in complex, interactive environments. Our goal is to enable LLMs to collaborate with humans and AI agents on long-horizon tasks.

Research Focus

Multi-Agent RL Environments

Build novel multi-agent environments where multiple LLMs and humans collaborate to solve tasks.

Human-AI Interaction

Train models to collaborate seamlessly with humans and other AI agents.

Continual Learning

Develop reinforcement learning algorithms to adapt and learn skills on the fly.

For more information, visit our GitHub repository or contact us at deltaintelligence@outlook.com.
